Provided by the Ministry for the environment, these are 21 actions anyone can do.

Drone Rules

Here, you can learn about operating drones on public conservation land and how to apply for a drone permit.
Drone use must be regulated to protect local wildlife and considers people's privacy.


Agricultural Emissions Calculator

This calculator provides a simple estimate of the greenhouse gases emitted and absorbed by your farm.
It works using the MfE 2022 Measuring Emissions Guidance, using the animals, fertiliser, and forest you have at your farm.
